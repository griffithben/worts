Who Are We?

If you are just starting out or have been brewing for years, we invite you to come hang out!

Events

Here's a list of all our upcoming events!

  • April 22

    Itap & Logboat Competition

    Do you have a winning recipe worthy of being brewed at Logboat and served at Itap? Sign up today to reserve your spot in the 3rd annual home brew competition!

  • April 29

    Morels & Microbrews

    Come to the historic Brick District of Fulton, MO on Saturday, April 29, 2017 from 1-5 pm for the 5th annual Morels & Microbrews Festival.

    SEC Beer Festival
