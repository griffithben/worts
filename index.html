Who Are We?

We are a group of misfit home brewers, having fun, brewing beer, taking names and kickin ass!

If you are just starting out or have been brewing for years, we invite you to come hang out.

Community

We are a group of homebrewers from all backgrounds helping each other out to learn more about this great craft.

Brewing Beer

Brewing beer is what we do. We regularly share brews and get together on group brew days. Lets get together and brew something awesome!

Learn Something New

Everyone has something to learn or share regardless of experience. Wether is be beer, mead, cider, wine or distilling. We want to learn about it!
